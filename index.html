ABOUT US

Over 50 regional and national obesity groups are represented by the World Obesity Federation, which unites professional representatives of the scientific, medical, and research sectors. World Obesity is a crucial partner with international obesity organizations, notably the World Health Organization.

The World Obesity Federation put forwards a global effort to diminish,prevent and treat obesity. It was founded in 1999.

The aim of the World Obesity Federationis to provide world-class training accessible to healthcare lifestyle.
